Privacy Policy for HueHop

Last updated: [UPDATE THIS DATE - e.g., December 15, 2024]

This Privacy Policy describes how HueHop ("we", "our", or "us") handles information in our mobile application. The app is developed by Josh Whitehouse.

Information We Collect

HueHop itself does not directly collect personal information from users. However, our third-party service providers may collect certain data as described below:

Advertising Data (Unity Ads)

We use Unity Ads to display advertisements in our app. Unity Ads may collect:

  • Device identifiers: Advertising ID, device model, operating system version
  • App usage information: Session duration, interactions with advertisements
  • Technical information: Screen size, device capabilities, network type
  • General location: Country or region-level location (not precise location)

Local Data Storage

The app stores the following information locally on your device only:

  • Game settings and preferences
  • High scores and achievements
  • Tutorial completion status

This local data is never transmitted off your device.

How We Use Information

Data collected by our service providers is used for:

  • Advertising: To display relevant advertisements
  • App functionality: To provide core game features
  • Performance: To ensure the app runs smoothly

Data Sharing

We share information with:

  • Unity Technologies: For advertising services (Unity Ads)
  • App stores: Apple App Store and Google Play Store for in-app purchases

We do not sell or share your personal information with other third parties.

Data Security

All data transmitted between the app and our service providers is encrypted using industry-standard security protocols (HTTPS/TLS). However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

In-App Purchases

Our app offers in-app purchases processed by Apple App Store or Google Play Store. All purchase transactions are handled directly by these platforms according to their respective privacy policies and terms of service.

Children's Privacy

Our app is designed for all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have inadvertently collected such information, please contact us immediately.

Your Choices

You can control data collection by:

  • Advertising ID: Reset or disable personalized ads in your device settings
  • Permissions: Manage app permissions in your device settings
  • Uninstall: Remove the app to stop all data collection

Third-Party Services

Our app uses the following third-party services:

  • Unity Ads: Unity Privacy Policy
  • Apple App Store: Apple Privacy Policy
  • Google Play Store: Google Privacy Policy

International Users

If you are located outside the United States, please note that information we collect may be transferred to and processed in the United States. By using our app, you consent to this transfer.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last updated" date. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.

Data Retention

Local data is retained on your device until you uninstall the app or manually clear the data. Data collected by third-party services is retained according to their respective privacy policies.
